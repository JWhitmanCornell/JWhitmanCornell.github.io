This dictionary is a work in progress.

Diacritics: At the moment, our dictionary does not include accents and underlines. You can include these in your search; they'll simply be ignored. If your search includes other diacritics (ę, ǫ, :, or ˀ), then only exact matches for those will be returned. If your search does not include diacritics, then results with any diacritics will also be returned. (For example, a search of "hono" could return words containing "ho:no," "honǫ," etc.)

Related words: The "related words" searches may not always accurately identify the stems of words. This means that some results may not actually be related.
